Linguini

Description

I don't know what linguini is. I probably won't ever need to. Linguini was one of the words in today's NYT Spelling Bee. I'm not a fan of the "ing" days, which they have like every other day.

Ingredients

  • Tall, thin, doughy cylinders
  • Parsley?
  • A Plate
  1. Grab lump of dough, roll into one long noodle
  2. Configure noodle like a ball of yarn
  3. Throw against wall, or ceiling
  4. Put it on a plate, may take some time to clean
